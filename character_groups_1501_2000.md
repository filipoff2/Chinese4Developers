# Chinese Character Groups (1501–2000 Range)
Grouped by Shared Components — Chinese → English

This file covers **top‑end professional language** used in:
platform teams, governance, compliance, incident management,
organizational scale, and decision‑making at enterprise level.

---

## 总结 — What 1501–2000 represents

This range represents **organizational‑scale language**, covering:
- governance & compliance
- executive‑level decision making
- platform sustainability
- ownership, accountability, economics

At this level, Chinese technical writing is **not about code** —
it is about **running systems and organizations**.
