# Chinese Character Groups — Learn by Shared Components
(Chinese → English)

Characters are grouped by **shared components (radicals)**.  
If you remember the **component meaning**, you can guess many characters.

---

## 人 / 亻 — Person, human action

Meaning: **a person doing something**

- 人 — person  
- 你 — you  
- 他 — he  
- 们 — people (plural marker)  
- 作 — do / make  
- 修 — fix  
- 使 — make / cause  
- 任 — assign / responsibility  
- 依 — depend  
- 供 — provide  

🧠 Tip: if you see **亻**, think  
> “someone does something”

---

## Summary: how to memorize

Instead of remembering:
> hundreds of isolated characters  

You remember:
> ~10 components + meaning

When you see a new character:
1. Identify the **component**
2. Guess the **semantic area**
3. Confirm by context  

That’s how native readers do it.
