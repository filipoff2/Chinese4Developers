# Chinese Character Groups (2001–2500 Range)
Grouped by Shared Components — Chinese → English

This range covers **enterprise‑scale thinking**:
portfolio management, platform economics, execution discipline,
organizational alignment, and long‑cycle system evolution.

---

- 战略 — strategy

- 战略布局 — strategic positioning

## 总结 — What 2001–2500 represents

This range represents **system‑of‑systems language**:
- portfolio & platform strategy  
- enterprise‑scale alignment  
- long‑cycle evolution  
- value, impact, and coordination

At this level, Chinese technical writing is about:
**direction, leverage, and sustainability**, not implementation details.
